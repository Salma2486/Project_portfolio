Classic Gameplay

Enjoy the timeless fun of the classic Snake game.

Leaderboard

Compete against yourself and others with built-in high scores tracking. Keep track of your best scores and strive to climb to the top of the leaderboard.

About


Growing up, I spent countless hours playing the classic Snake game on my old Nokia phone. The simple yet addictive gameplay always kept me entertained during long car rides or lazy afternoons. When I embarked on my journey into game development, I knew I wanted to pay homage to this beloved childhood favorite.

Creator

I've poured my passion and dedication into every aspect of its development. From designing the gameplay mechanics to coding the user interface, every element of the game reflects my vision and creativity.

You can explore the code for this project on GitHub.


How to play?

In this Snake game remake, players control a snake that grows longer as it eats food. The objective is to navigate the snake around the game board, avoiding collisions with the walls and itself
